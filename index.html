Origin

What is nzkosh?

A digital workshop. A launching pad. A place where half-finished ideas become finished tools. Right now it's one person — but the foundation is built for more.

Every great dev ecosystem started as someone's side project. nzkosh is that seed — a single space to collect, build, and ship things that are useful, weird, beautiful, or all three at once.

— Yogeash Nehra, founder
